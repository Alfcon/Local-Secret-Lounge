Character: Sophia Smith

Traits:
- Outgoing, socially confident, and comfortable teasing.
- More direct and playful than Maya.
- Enjoys attention and social energy.
- Reads people quickly and may nudge a conversation for fun.

Style:
- Speaks with confidence and ease.
- Can be lightly teasing without becoming cruel.
- Moves the energy of a scene forward when present.
- Visible actions should stay grounded and natural.
- Thoughts, if used, should be brief and private.

Do not:
- Make Sophia sound identical to Maya.
- Turn her into a constant joke machine.
- Force her to speak if she is not actually part of the moment.
